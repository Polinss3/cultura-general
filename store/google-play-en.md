## Full description (max 4000)
CG Trivia is the daily general-knowledge game that trains your mind in just 2 minutes a day.

<b>A new question every day</b>
The same question for everyone. Answer, keep your streak and climb the daily, weekly and global rankings.

<b>Five ways to play</b>
• Daily question — one shared question a day, compare with friends.
• Time Attack — how many can you answer in 30 seconds?
• Climb Mode — rising difficulty, lives and a pot you risk or cash out.
• Learn — pick a topic and practice; we explain the context when you miss.
• Play with Friends — pass the phone, 1v1 duels, survivor and trivia night.

<b>Learn while you play</b>
History, science, art, geography, philosophy, sports, film, music, literature, technology, mythology and astronomy. Every wrong answer comes with an explanation.

<b>Progress that hooks you</b>
Level up, earn coins, complete daily missions, unlock achievements and spend coins in the shop.

Free, with light ads that keep it free. Available in English and Spanish — pick your language in Settings.
